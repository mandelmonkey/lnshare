⚡ Clear LNShare Cache

Quick Fix

✅ Cache cleared! Reloading...

Manual Steps (iOS Safari)

  1. Close the LNShare app completely
  2. Open Settings app
  3. Scroll down to Safari
  4. Tap Advanced
  5. Tap Website Data
  6. Search for lnshare
  7. Swipe left and tap Delete
  8. Go back and revisit the app

Manual Steps (Android Chrome)

  1. Long-press the LNShare app icon
  2. Tap App info
  3. Tap Storage
  4. Tap Clear storage and Clear cache
  5. Go back and revisit the app

Alternative: Force Refresh

  1. Open the app in your browser (not as PWA)
  2. iOS: Pull down to refresh
  3. Android: Pull down to refresh, or long-press reload button
  4. The new version should load
